# 🧐 About Me

Xinhang Wan (万欣航) is a lecturer with the National Key Laboratory of Information Systems Engineering, College of Systems Engineering, National University of Defense Technology (NUDT), China. He received the Ph.D. degree from the National University of Defense Technology in 2025, supervised by Prof. [Xinwang Liu](https://xinwangliu.github.io/) and Prof. En Zhu in the Pattern Recognition & Machine Intelligence Lab (PRMI).

**Research Interests**: Affordance learning, Novel class discovery, Multi-view Learning, Continual Learning, Active Learning, Semi-supervised Classification, Clustering.
